    American Idol Season 20 winner Noah Thompson just announced that he’s going on a co-headlining tour with runner-up HunterGirl later this year. The shows will kick off in October, with dates running into December.

    Noah Thompson, HunterGirl Announce Tour Together

    On Wednesday, Noah and HunterGirl both announced the tour on their social media pages, with a list of dates starting with October 27 in Columbus, Ohio. They’ll also travel to Illinois, Missouri, Pennsylvania, Indiana, and Kentucky.

    “I can’t believe a year ago, I was working construction, and now I’m announcing a tour with one of my closest friends, HunterGirl,” Noah said in a press release. “HunterGirl and I met on Idol and became really close. We’ve stayed friends, and now we get to get out there on the road together. One of my favorite things to do is get up on stage and sing. I am literally living in my dream world.”

    Meanwhile, HunterGirl teased that she’ll be releasing new music soon, saying, “It is going to be so much fun being on tour with one of my good friends, Noah Thompson. I am excited to be on the road with my band and have the chance to play new music that will be coming out soon.” 

    Pre-sale for tickets to the tour starts on Thursday, and public sale starts on Friday. Tickets are available through either of the singers’ sites, here and here.

    The Singers Performed Together Earlier This Month

    This tour won’t be the country singers’ first time performing together. Earlier this month, they both took the stage in Michigan. Before that, they performed at Whiskey Jam in Nashville, along with several other past American Idol stars, including Fritz Hager.

    Noah was named the winner of Season 20 earlier this year, with HunterGirl coming in second place. Recently, Noah released a cover of Rihanna’s “Stay,” which he also performed during his time on the show.

    Meanwhile, HunterGirl has been teasing new music on her social media, particularly a song called “Hometown Out of Me.” The singer has already shared more than on snippet from the track. Hopefully it will be released ahead of her tour with Noah.
