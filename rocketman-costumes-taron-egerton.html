    Rocketman Costume Designer on Recreating Elton Johns Iconic Outfits

    When it came to recreating Elton Johns iconic outfits in Rocketman, costume designer Julian Day visited the singers personal archives to see the details on his colorful clothing up close. Day also used authentic jewelry from Chopard, including three watches decked out with real diamonds that came out to a million dollars.
