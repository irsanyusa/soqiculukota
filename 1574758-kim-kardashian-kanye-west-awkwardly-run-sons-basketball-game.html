    Kim Kardashian, Ex-hubby Kanye West Awkwardly Run Into Each Other at Son's Basketball Game

    Award-winning American rapper Kim Kardashian and his ex-wife, reality TV star Kim Kardashian, were spotted together at their son Saint's basketball game. The ex-lovers had a public spat in the past, accusing each other of making co-parenting harder for the other.

    21 Jun 2024, 00:00
    • Kanye West and Kim Kardashian had in the past publicly accused each other of sabotaging co-parenting after getting divorced
    • However, the ex-lovers reunited at their son Saint's basketball game, and neither seemed to be bothered by the other's presence
    • Kim filed for divorce from Kanye in February 2021 after being married for seven years, and their split was officially finalised in November 2022

    Award-winning American rapper Kim Kardashian and his ex-wife, reality TV star Kim Kardashian, were spotted together at their son Saint's basketball game.

    The ex-lovers had a public spat in the past, accusing each other of making co-parenting harder for the other.

    Kanye once lashed out at the Kardashian family, accusing them of denying him access to his children while pointing out that he has missed out on some special moments, including his children's birthdays.

    However, Kim denied the claims and noted that her ex-husband Kanye was lying and accused him of playing a narrative.

    Inside Kanye West, Kim Kardashian's reunion

    According to Page Six, the mother of four ran into Kanye, where they both showed up to support their son, who had a basketball match.

    They had not been seen or photographed together even as they were still navigating their co-parenting woes.

    From the photos, Kim appeared to stay cordial while engaging with her ex as their daughter Chicago, 6, stood between them.

    The Golddigger crooner wore a black t-shirt with his back facing the camera while his ex, Kim, faced him, looking low-key in a casual baseball cap and a white tank top.

    However, Kim looked busy texting on her phone while standing next to Kanye, who had his full attention on the game.

    Kim vows to never go back to Kanye

    Elsewhere, the mother of four maintained she was not planning on ever returning to her ex-husband, Kanye.

    Kim praised Kanye as a loving dad while also vowing she would never separate him from his kids.

    She delved deep into her ended marriage while remembering the memories she still had while clearing out her storage warehouse.

    "I’m really at peace with it. There are so many amazing things that came out of my marriage and my divorce. I’ve kept every single Yeezy. For me, I think this is me holding on to the Kanye I know," she said.
