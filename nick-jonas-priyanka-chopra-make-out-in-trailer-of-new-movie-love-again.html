    Nick Jonas has repeatedly won “husband of the year” when it comes to his support of wife Priyanka Chopra. This time, Jonas shared Chopra’s newest film role and even shared his experience in the film. In Chopra’s upcoming film Love Again, the couple has a steamy, and partially awkward makeout scene.

    Nick Jonas Makes Hilarious Cameo in Wife Priyanka Chopra’s Upcoming Movie

    Love Again is a romantic comedy movie featuring Priyanka Chopra and Outlander actor Sam Heughan. The storyline basically begins when Chopra’s character Mira Ray begins sending endearing text messages to his dead fiance’s old cell phone number, which she believes to have already been defunct. It turns out that Heughan’s character Rob Burns becomes at the receiving end of the texts upon getting a new work phone. As Rob continues to obtain her messages, he finds himself falling in love with a woman he never even met.

    While the whole trailer beautifully delivered, viewers particularly noticed a scene where Chopra’s real-life lover appeared. Interestingly, Love Again has shot a hilarious taxi make out session between Jonas and Chopra. On Instagram, the “Jealous” singer reflected on the said cameo.

    “Glad our first date went better than this one Priyanka Chopra,” he wrote. “Loved having the chance to see you shine on set and can’t wait for the world to see this movie!”

    Evidently, Jonas will not be Chopra’s endgame in this movie. Nonetheless, this movie will still be remembered as the first movie they’ve been with together. In addition, this film is Celine Dion’s film debut.

     Fans Are Happy to See Jonas’s Support For His Wife

    Aside from the movie itself, fans are also commending Jonas’s wholesome support for his wife. Several Instagram users have affirmed that Chopra “married the right guy.”

    “Gotta love how nick supports his wife in everything she does,” one fan wrote.

    A few fans apparently wish that Jonas could’ve been the Indian actress’s leading man. Nevertheless, several are excited for the on-screen chemistry between Heughan and Chopra. Some fans, on the other hand, can’t help but point out how comical Jonas’s cameo is in the movie.

    “You got me I randomly burst into laughter and got quite the stares!!!!” another user wrote.

    Love Again will be out in the cinemas on Mother’s Day, May 12, 2023.
