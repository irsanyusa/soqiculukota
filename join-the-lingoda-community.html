    Lingoda isn’t just Europe’s largest online language school, it’s also an online community of thousands of people across the globe. From teachers, to students, to writers and editors. Read on to find out how you can connect with them.

    How can I join the Lingoda community?

    From the moment you sign up to take a Lingoda free trial or you purchase a package, you will have access to the student back-end. Here you can take classes at any time with students from over 100 countries across the globe. All of our classes are live and with native-speaking, qualified teachers. Once you have taken a few of our online classes, you will start to recognize students in your language level, as well as the teachers. Slowly but surely, you’ll create a rapport with your peers and the teachers, and that’s when the real fun begins! You will be able to help your classmates and see each other grow in your understanding of your chosen language.

    Social media communities

    Facebook

    If you’re on social media, you can take advantage of our communities. Taking the Lingoda Sprint? Then you can access our Facebook group of current and past Sprint students who are there to give you a helping hand and any encouragement and tips you may need!

    Join the Lingoda Sprint Facebook Group

    Twitter

    Do you love to Tweet? Then join our 20 thousand followers on Twitter and let us know what you think about Lingoda. We’d also love to know any tips you have for learning languages at home. We are always happy to hear from you!

    Follow Lingoda on Twitter

    Instagram

    Fancy yourself as a bit of a photographer, or do you love seeing an aesthetically pleasing pic? Over on our Instagram, we have over 50 thousand followers who love to comment and chat on our profile. From educational videos to language learning tips and inspiration from our staff and influencers, you can really engage with like-minded learners.

    Follow Lingoda on Instagram

    YouTube

    With interviews from some of the world’s biggest influencers such as English with Lucy and superholly, to fun language learning tips and educational lessons, Lingoda’s YouTube will really help and support your online lessons.

    Subscribe to the Lingoda YouTube channel

    LinkedIn

    Looking for something a little more professional? Visit the Lingoda LinkedIn for updates on our promotions, vacancies, and more. Our community of over 8 thousand people is growing every day.
