    Colin Stough, the talented singer who gained recognition through American Idol Season 21, recently announced his new EP, Promiseland. The post, shared on his official Instagram account, featured a captivating caption that left fans eagerly anticipating his industry breakthrough.

    Colin Stough Announces New EP Promiseland

    The announcement was accompanied by a striking visual showing two road signs that hinted at the EP’s thematic elements. This suggests a captivating musical adventure that listeners can look forward to.

    Stough‘s journey on American Idol Season 21 earned him a loyal fan base. In addition, his talent and charisma left a lasting impact on viewers and judges alike. Despite not making it to the final two of the competition, Stough’s soulful renditions and heartfelt performances struck a chord with audiences.

    Since his time on the show, Stough has been working hard on his music career, and the upcoming EP Promiseland is a testament to his dedication and artistry. Fans can expect a unique blend of musical styles and heartfelt lyrics that reflect Stough’s personal journey and experiences.

    The EP is scheduled for release on September 8, and fans can already pre-save the music to ensure they are among the first to experience Stough’s latest musical endeavor. With the caption’s reference to Promiseland and the intriguing visual, it’s clear that Stough is inviting his listeners to join him on a musical voyage filled with emotions, stories, and melodies.

    Colin Stough Adds Six More Shows to His Ongoing Tour

    Following the anticipation surrounding his upcoming EP release, Stough has announced six new tour dates. Each date promises to bring his soulful melodies and captivating performances to fans across various cities.

    On September 21, Stough will be gracing the stage at Wendell’s Dippin Branch in Anderson, South Carolina, treating the audience to an intimate evening of his heartfelt music. The following day, September 22, he will continue his tour at Soul Kitchen in Mobile, Alabama, where fans can experience his vocals up close and personal.

    As October arrives, Stough’s tour then gains momentum with two exciting performances. On October 12, he will take the stage at Barrelhouse Ballroom in Chattanooga, Tennessee, followed by a performance on October 13 at the iconic 40 Watt Club in Athens, Georgia.

    The tour’s excitement keeps building as Stough heads to Little Rock, Arkansas, for a performance at the Revolution Music Room on October 20. To wrap up the series of newly announced shows, he’ll also be hitting Brewsky’s in Hattiesburg, Mississippi, on October 21. Tickets are now available via Bands in Town.
