    Educational background

    She has a Business administration degree from the University of Kent. She also attended the Berklee College of Music.

    Career

    Tiwa first worked at Royal Bank of Scotland after graduating from the university before she went into music full-time.

    She had a passion for music from a young age. She played trombone in the local orchestra band when she was in high school.

    She got the opportunity to be a backup vocalist for the legendary singer George Michael when she was 16 years old.

    She also worked as background vocal for many other prominent musicians, for instance, Mary J. Blige, Kelly Clarkson, Emma Bunton, Andrea Bocelli, and others.

    She has also performed on the same stage as 50 Cent, Eminem, Destiny’s Child, Robbie Williams, Sting, and The Black Eyed Peas.

    2006 was the year when Savage became a participant of the X-Factor show in the United Kingdom. She showed reasonably good results in the competition and managed to advance to the final 24, but she did not make it to the live shows.

    Her participation in the show coincided with her studies at Berklee College of Music. The musician had difficulties with suddenly being in the limelight and meeting people’s expectations.

    Savage also said that she was really upset when she did not make it to the final 12, but she tried her best to contain her emotions because she wanted to be an example and role model for the people who were watching her.

    They appreciated her amazing songwriting talent and hired her to write songs. Some examples of musicians she has written songs for include Kat Deluna, Mya, Monica, and Babyface. Tiwa also wrote Monica’s beautiful ballad Catch Me.
