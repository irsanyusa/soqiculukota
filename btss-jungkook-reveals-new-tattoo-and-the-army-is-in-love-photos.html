    On a new episode of Run BTS!, the ARMY got a glimpse of Jungkook’s latest tattoo. The web series allowed fans to get a close-up of all of his arm tattoos which is very rare. As you can imagine, everyone is totally freaking out on Twitter and shared photos of Jungkook’s new ink.

    BTS Web Series Shows Off Jungkook’s New Tattoo

    In this episode, the members of the world’s biggest South Korean boyband cooked a pot of ham and potato stew but most fans could not focus on anything other than Jungkook’s tattoos. The ARMY spotted a new microphone tattoo on his arm after he rolled his sleeves up during the episode and are hoping to get a full glimpse of all of his tattoos in the future.

    Jungkook has some pretty famous ink, with several of his tattoos taking on a deep meaning. He also has a tattoo of a red eye, a skeleton hand making the “rock on” gesture along with the word “ARMY” written across his knuckles. This tattoo is to pay tribute to the incredible BTS fanbase that has been supportive since the very beginning. He also has a purple heart and the ARMY symbol on the back of his hand.

    He has a tattoo of Chinese symbols. They were used by BTS for their trilogy called “The Most Beautiful Moment In Life.” The symbols translate to “The era of flourishing.” It is a tattoo to commemorate the success and beautiful moments that the group has had thus far in their already booming careers. This is of course thanks to the support of the ARMY.

    Others shared mesmerizing gifs of all of the glimpses of Jungkook’s tattoos.

    If you are just as obsessed with BTS as we are, check out the What’s Hot Video below. Host Mriganka talks about how they have become so successful in America and have taken over the music scene.

    He Recently Won ‘Top Tattoo’ At The 2020 TDY Awards

    Jungkook also was just named as the winner of the Top Tattoo Award during the TDY Awards. He is believed to have around 20 tattoos in total.

    “He Really Has The Most Beautiful And Meaningful Tattoos! They Become Way More Special When You Know The Meaning And Think That He Just Didn’t Decide To Do Them Because He Liked Them!! I Hope Everyone Can Respect It And He Can Talk About Them And Show Them!” one ARMY member wrote on Twitter.

    Others urged people not to copy Jungkook’s tattoos since they are unique to him. “Please please PLEASE don’t copy Jungkook’s tattoos. I’m sure every tattoo has a meaning to him and will only make him uncomfortable if y’all copy them. Respect his boundaries!!” another ARMY member wrote on Twitter.

    “I love that you can see the love he has for music in every single thing he does, his tattoos, his career, his hobbies. He has a tattoo of a microphone, a G-clef, the lyrics to his favorite song & probably more music related references” wrote another person on Twitter.
