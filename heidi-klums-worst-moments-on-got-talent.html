    As a judge on various seasons and spin-offs of America’s Got Talent, Heidi Klum has witnessed countless extraordinary performances. Still, she has also been part of some moments that left audiences scratching their heads. While her insights and critiques have shaped the show’s dynamic, her time on AGT is not without its missteps. Let’s delve into Heidi Klum’s 8 Worst Moments on America’s Got Talent.

    Heidi Klum’s 8 Worst Moments on America’s Got Talent

    8. Standing Ovation Snub for Courtney Hadwin – AGT Season 13

    In AGT season 13, finalist Courtney Hadwin took the stage for her Semi-Finals performance of “Born to Be Wild” by Steppenwolf. While Simon Cowell and Howie Mandel were quick to rise in a standing ovation, both Mel B and Klum remained seated. This moment stirred a wave of discontent among fans who believed Courtney’s performance was deserving of unanimous acclaim.

    7. The Holiday Tumble That Sparked Speculation – AGT: A Holiday of Champions

    During the festive TV special AGT: A Holiday of Champions, Klum and former AGT contestant Sal Valentinetti took the stage for a lively rendition of “Santa Baby.” However, the highlight of their performance was unexpected: Klum appeared to fake a fall while singing. The incident left viewers questioning whether it was a genuine mishap or a staged moment. Furthermore, Klum and Valentinetti have remained tight-lipped about the incident, fueling further speculation.

    6. Klum’s Buzz for Unconventional Act – AGT Season 18

    During Season 18 of America’s Got Talent, Klum found herself in the midst of controversy after she buzzed an unconventional act. The act featured two performers dressed as the horror movie character Michael Myers, captivating the audience with a combination of piano and dance.

    However, Klum pressed her buzzer prematurely, indicating her disapproval before the act had concluded. Fans of the show took to social media to criticize her judgment, with some suggesting that her sense of humor might be better suited elsewhere.

    5. April Fools’ Pregnancy Prank Sparks Backlash – AGT 2023

    Klum found herself in hot water after an April Fools’ Day prank on Instagram raised eyebrows and sparked backlash. The 50-year-old supermodel decided to playfully fool her followers by pretending to be pregnant, sharing a photo backstage at AGT 2023 where a faux bump protruded from her attire while posing with her fellow AGT judge, Sofia Vergara. Although the prank was intended to be light-hearted, fans and commenters criticized Klum for insensitivity, particularly for those dealing with infertility or miscarriages.

    4. Controversy Over Klum’s Critique of Plus-Sized Contestant – AGT Season 15

    Season 15 of AGT stirred controversy when Klum faced backlash for her critique of a young plus-sized competitor, Amanda LaCount. Despite sharing her journey of facing rejection due to her body type and the hope to change perceptions, Klum hit her buzzer only 40 seconds into the act.

    Many believed that Klum’s actions crossed the line, implying that LaCount’s appearance didn’t meet the show’s standards. The incident ignited discussions about body shaming and the judging criteria on the show.

    3. Mixed Reactions to Klum’s Golden Buzzer Choice – AGT Season 17

    Season 17 of AGT saw Lily Meola capturing the attention of the judges, particularly Klum. Klum was moved by Meola’s emotional performance and decided to press the Golden Buzzer, granting her direct entry to the live shows. However, fans were divided about the choice, as Meola became the third singer to receive a Golden Buzzer that season. Disappointment arose among viewers who wished to see more diverse and unique acts highlighted through the coveted Golden Buzzer, rather than singers who share personal stories.

    2. Howie Mandel Playfully Exposes Klum’s “Bad Breath” – AGT 2023

    During a behind-the-scenes Q&A, the judges were playing a game of revealing truths. When it was Klum’s turn to ask a question, Howie Mandel interrupted to shade Klum’s alleged bad breath. Vergara laughed, while Klum covered her mouth in mild embarrassment. Klum later admitted that she consumes garlic soup weekly as part of her health regimen.

    1. Klum Called a Contestant’s Ex-Wife – AGT Season 18

    Season 18 of AGT took an unexpected turn during the sixth round of auditions when Klum shocked everyone by calling a contestant’s ex-wife in the middle of his audition. Firefighter Trent Toney from Oregon was on the stage to perform a song he had written for his ex-wife, hoping to win her back. Klum, intrigued by the situation, asked Toney to bring his phone and then proceeded to video call Faith, his ex-wife.

    Fans and viewers found the entire scenario “weird” and questioned the emotional manipulation of the situation. The judges applauded Toney’s bravery for bringing such personal matters to the national stage, but viewers expressed concerns about the authenticity of the moment and whether it was fair to involve Faith in this way during an audition.
