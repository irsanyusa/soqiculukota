    The Masked Singer season 4 has promised, and delivered, bigger and better stars than ever! The Masked Singer Snow Owls shocked everyone as the first duo in the show’s history. And when it was time for them to sing, they brought their A game!

    But which celebrities are under the Snow Owl masks? Here are all the clues, plus our prediction!

    Clues on The Masked Singer Season 4

    In the Super Sneak Peak, the Snow Owls didn’t provide any real clues. They just said, “You can try and figure out who we are, but as for who is more talented, it’s a tie.”

    The Snow Owls’ premiere episode clues take place in “JAM-A-LOT.” They say “you haven’t seen us together in a while,” as they fly over to a couch with a clam and pearl on a table next to them. Now, “it’s time for a family reunion!” The female snow owl explains that the male is a prankster, “a real wise guy.” We see a rose squirt water, then signs reading “MAGNOLIA” and “QUARREL CANYON.” They tell a story about the male singing “Let It Snow” off-key, then pulling up to their Hollywood house, where he had gotten it covered in snow for Christmas.

    Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Snow Owls

    Robin thought it might be Amy Grant and Vince Gill. Jenny guessed Maureen McCormack and Barry Williams or Donnie and Marie Osmond. Ken predicted Eugene Levy and Catherine O’Hara. Guesses on the internet are pretty in line with the judges, while also adding in Julianne and Derek Hough, Chip and Joanna Gaines, Clint Black and Lisa Hartman Black, and Garth Brooks and Trisha Yearwood. For a costume with two voices, the predictions have been all over the place! This is not an easy one.

    Our Prediction for The Snow Owls On The Masked Singer

    Between the voices and the few clues we got from their first packages, we finally have an idea of who the Snow Owls are. They are none other than…

    Married singers, Clint Black and Lisa Hartman Black!

    These country lovebirds are the perfect candidates for the Snow Owls. The clues line up too! Here’s what they all mean.

    As far as them saying “it’s a tie” for who’s the most talented, Clint and Lisa both have successful careers. The pearl and clam next to the couch in the clues is a reference to their child, Lily Pearl. We also saw “QUARREL CANYON” in the clues, which is alluding to the fact that they used to live in Laurel Canyon. And the house at Christmas is a nod to Clint’s Christmas albums and singles.

    The discussion about the male being goofy make sense for Clint Black and Lisa Hartman Black too. Clint is a known jokester! And at the end of the day, The Masked Singer Snow Owls’ voices are just a perfect match for the couple. Though it does seem that they might have left a little country twang behind for their cover of “Say Something.”
